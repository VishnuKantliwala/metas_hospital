about us

Metas Adventist Hospital, Surat is one of the oldest and well known organizations with its wings spread in the fields of education and health care system. It’s a like gem studded in the crown of Seventh – Day Adventist World organization. Some important facts about this institution are as described below.

Seventh Day Adventists – Worldwide out of 815 Seventh Day Adventist operated hospitals, 80% of them are JCI approved.

In 1985 the first infant heart transplant was done at Loma Linda University which is world renowned for pediatric cardiac surgery.
